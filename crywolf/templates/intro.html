What to expect

You will take a brief questionnaire, then begin a training exercise to familiarize yourself with the IDS and how to evaluate an alert. Once training is complete, you will begin the experiment. The experiment will look exactly like the training, but there will be more alerts to evaluate. Once you have evaluated the events in the experiment, click the "Finished!" button at the bottome of the page. This will take you to another brief questionnaire asking for feedback on the experiment. Finally, you will be provided with a completion code that you will need to write down on your login card an submit to the proctor.
