Welcome to the IDS Simulator!

The Scenario

You are a junior cyber security analyst at Company XYZ. You perform initial triage on incoming network events that your company's Intrusion Detection System (IDS) has deemed an alert. Your job is to evaluate alerts to determine if they could constitute an attack against the company — thus needing to be escalated to a higher level echelon of analysis — or is harmless network activity and can be ignored.

What to expect

After this page you will take a brief questionnaire, then begin a training exercise to familiarize yourself with the IDS and how to evaluate an alert. Once training is complete, you will begin the experiment. The experiment will look exactly like the training, but there will be more alerts to evaluate. Once you have evaluated the events in the experiment, click the "Finished!" button at the bottome of the page. This will take you to brief survey asking for feedback on the experiment. Submitting the survey completes the experiment, and you will be provided with a completion code that you will need to write down on your login card.
